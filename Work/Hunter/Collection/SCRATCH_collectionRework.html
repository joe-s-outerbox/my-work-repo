Design and Function

  • Efficiency: Hunter Industrial HVLS fans are engineered to move large volumes of air at low speeds. This helps in circulating air more efficiently, reducing the burden on cooling and heating systems and ultimately leading to energy savings.
  • Aerodynamics: HVLS ceiling fans incorporate a unique blade design that produces greater airflow with less energy consumption. By enacting the principles of aerodynamics, the fan creates optimal air distribution and minimal drag.
  • Safety: With its robust and sturdy construction, an HVLS fan is built to last and withstand industrial conditions. Its safety features ensure that it functions reliably without posing risks to the environments in which it operates.
  • Compliance: Our HVLS fans conform to ANSI/AMCA regulations and guidance for large-diameter ceiling fans.

Versatility

We offer a range of industrial HVLS fans suitable for various applications. This versatility ensures that businesses of all sizes and types benefit from optimized air circulation.

Ease of Use and Maintenance

With direct drive motors, our HVLS ceiling fans eliminate the need for gears, resulting in fewer parts wearing out or requiring maintenance. This design choice also ensures quieter operation. In addition, the user-friendly controls and systems allow for straightforward operations and adjustments, making it easier for facility managers to regulate and maintain desired conditions.

Eco-Friendliness

By reducing the dependence on HVAC systems, industrial HVLS fans lower carbon footprints. Not only do they use less energy themselves, but they also help maintain consistent temperatures, which further diminishes energy consumption.

Benefits to Businesses

  • Cost Savings: As they reduce the need for air conditioning or heating, businesses will witness significant savings in energy bills.
  • Improved Comfort: For facilities that house employees, a more consistent and comfortable temperature boosts productivity and morale.
  • Protection for Products: In industries where goods are affected by humidity or temperature fluctuations – like agriculture or certain types of manufacturing – an HVLS fan helps protect products by ensuring a stable environment.

The versatility and efficiency of an HVLS fan from Hunter Industrial make it a highly sought-after solution across diverse commercial and industrial landscapes. Let's explore the unique benefits they offer in these pivotal sectors:

Warehouses

Large storage spaces like warehouses often suffer from uneven temperature zones. HVLS ceiling fans help circulate air uniformly, maintaining consistent temperatures and ensuring goods are stored under optimal conditions. With their ability to move vast volumes of air, these fans significantly reduce the reliance on HVAC systems, leading to considerable energy savings.

Automotive Facilities

Automotive workshops are prone to dust accumulation. Industrial HVLS fans assist in settling airborne particles, thus maintaining a cleaner working environment. Meanwhile, mechanics and other staff benefit from the enhanced air circulation, leading to reduced heat stress and increased productivity.

Manufacturing

In certain manufacturing processes, high humidity hinders product quality. An HVLS fan helps reduce moisture content in the air, thereby preserving the integrity of the manufactured items. By ensuring proper ventilation, these fans also help dissipate potentially harmful fumes or gases that might be present in some manufacturing settings.

Aviation

Hangars, repair bays, and other aviation facilities greatly benefit from the large air displacements of HVLS ceiling fans, ensuring optimal conditions for maintenance and repair tasks. Proper air circulation prevents the buildup of moisture, which is essential in aeronautical settings to avoid corrosion of aircraft components.
