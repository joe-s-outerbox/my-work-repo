A Hunter Air Circulators Pedestal Fan + an HVLS Fan = A Match Made in Heaven

The Hunter Air Circulators pedestal fan collaborates seamlessly with High-Volume, Low-Speed (HVLS) fans to establish a proficient and efficient airflow solution. HVLS fans, developed to circulate air in expansive settings like warehouses, lower the overall temperature and sustain a consistent climate. However, their configuration, ideal for promoting generalized air movement on a large scale, might not be as effective in addressing smaller, confined areas needing specific cooling or ventilation.

This is where our Hunter Air Circulators Pedestal Fan comes into its own. Equipped with a powerful motor and focused airflow, it can accurately target and cool specific zones requiring additional ventilation, such as workstations, machinery, or spots prone to heat buildup. Given its portability, a standing fan is conveniently relocated around your workspace, delivering adaptable and localized air movement right where it's needed.

While the HVLS fan provides cooling for a wide area, like a whole manufacturing facility, and ensures a uniform temperature, our Pedestal Fans take care of any remaining hotspots. Using horse barns as an example, the synergistic effect of these two fans guarantees that the entire barn area and the horse stalls within it benefit from effective and optimal air circulation. This dual approach generates a more comfortable and safer work environment, enhancing productivity and well-being among workers in automotive shops, warehouses, factories, and other industrial settings.
