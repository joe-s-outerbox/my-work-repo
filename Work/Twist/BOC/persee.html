Crafting Beauty With Intent

Nawal Laoui, the creative force behind Persée, infuses her pieces with a deep respect for the ancient legends that inspire her while embracing modern aesthetics. The artistry of Persée is rooted in the idea of freedom – freedom for each stone to express its natural beauty and for each wearer to express their individual style. Some of the hallmarks of Persée jewelry include:

  • Pierced Diamond Technique: A signature Japanese method that allows diamonds to float ethereally on the skin.
  • Minimalist Aesthetic: Clean, sober lines emphasizing the stone's natural brilliance.
  • Mythical Inspiration: Elements drawn from the story of Perseus and Danae, blending ancient lore with contemporary design.
  • Global Artistic Influences: Inspired by Nawal's travels and the works of Picasso, Calder and Brancusi.
  • Focus on Purity: Highlighting the natural beauty of unadorned diamonds, sapphires, rubies, emeralds and other gemstones.
  • Sensory Experience: Jewelry designed to be as delightful to touch as it is to behold.

Where Did the Name Persée Come From?

The name Persée is rich in mythical and cultural significance. It was chosen by Nawal Laoui not only for its association with the grandiose king Perseus, son of Zeus and Danae in Greek mythology but also for its resonance with a rare "piercing" technique used in Japan. This double symbolism reflects Laoui's desire to infuse her jewelry with powerful narratives and historical depth while highlighting the innovative craftsmanship behind each piece.

How Is the Pierced Diamond Technique Achieved?

This signature technique involves laser-drilling diamonds to allow them to be attached to jewelry with a delicate ring, creating an illusion of the diamond floating on the skin.

What Materials Are Featured in Persée Jewelry?

Persée jewelry is composed of diamonds or precious and semi-precious color gemstones in minimalist settings of 18-karat yellow gold.

How does Persée Jewelry look so invisibly crafted?

Wearing Persee jewelry is like wearing a mere suggestion of the jewelry form. There are no cumbersome findings or mountings used in Persée jewelry. Just finely cut stones that look suspended in air like drops of ice. Designer jewelry by Persée is known for its restraint and elegance.

What makes Persée Designer Jewelry so Distinct?

Wearing Persée Jewelry is the opposite of statement jewelry. What makes Persée jewelry so different has as much to do with what it’s not as what it is. Persée jewelry is often worn as a layer of jewelry with other pieces as an understated accent.

Who Wears Persée Jewelry?
