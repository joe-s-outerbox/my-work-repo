The Innovation Behind Moritz Glik

Celebrate innovation with Moritz Glik Jewelry, where the visionary designer himself crafts a world of possibilities. Moritz Glik, a master jeweler with a penchant for pushing the envelope, infuses his creations with a dynamic blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary flair.

As a designer, Moritz Glik draws inspiration from diverse facets of life, fusing art, architecture, and his own avant-garde sensibilities. His background in engineering shines through in the meticulous construction of each piece, creating jewelry that is not just an accessory but a wearable work of art.

Moritz Glik Jewelry: Expect the Unexpected

Through the incorporation of unconventional elements, Moritz Glik is a one-of-a-kind designer within the industry. The signature "Kaleidoscope" collection, featuring loose diamonds, emeralds and sapphires encased in double sapphire domes, is a testament to his groundbreaking approach. His pieces come to life with every movement, capturing the essence of fluidity and constant transformation. Glik’s jewelry often features:

  • Shaker Technique: Moritz Glik's signature design is revolutionary, featuring gems encased in sapphire crystals, allowing them to move freely and catch light from all angles, creating a mesmerizing effect.
  • Hand-selected Gemstones: Only the finest gems make it into a Moritz Glik piece. Each stone is carefully chosen for its clarity, color and brilliance.
  • 18K Yellow Gold Settings: A signature element in Moritz Glik jewelry is the use of 18K yellow gold, chosen for its warmth and richness. This gold setting complements the brilliance of the encased stones and adds a luxurious finish to each piece.
  • Artistic Vision: Moritz Glik's personal touch is evident in each creation, reflecting his passion for jewelry that's both imaginative and expressive.
  • Precision Craftsmanship: From the initial sketch to the final polish, every aspect of Moritz Glik jewelry is considered. Every piece is handcrafted with utmost precision, ensuring impeccable design execution.
  • Innovative Designs: Moritz Glik's designs seamlessly blend classical elegance with a modern twist, creating timeless pieces that are both contemporary and deeply rooted in traditional jewelry-making.

Awards & Accolades for Moritz Glik

The exceptional artistry of Moritz Glik has captivated jewelry enthusiasts and earned recognition in the fashion and jewelry industry. His innovative designs have been featured prominently in prestigious publications, including Vogue, INSTORE and W magazine. Additionally, JCK, a leading authority in jewelry news, highlighted Glik's creations. Beyond the glossy pages, Moritz Glik's statement pieces have become a favorite among celebrities, often seen adorning stars at high-profile events, capturing the attention and admiration of photographers and fashion critics alike.

Moritz Glik's exceptional skill and creative vision have been acknowledged and celebrated within the jewelry industry. His remarkable achievements include being honored as a two-time recipient of The Couture Show's prestigious Best Diamond Piece Award, a feat he accomplished consecutively in 2009 and 2010. Further solidifying his status as a distinguished designer, in 2016, Moritz Glik was the proud recipient of InStyle magazine's Inaugural InDesign Award in the category of Diamond Designs.

These accolades are a testament to Glik's dedication to excellence and his innovative approach to jewelry design.

How Is Moritz Glik Jewelry Crafted?

Each piece of Moritz Glik Jewelry is handcrafted, ensuring one-of-a-kind creations. His work involves a meticulous process of selection, design and assembly, reflecting a high standard of craftsmanship and attention to detail. The Kaleidoscope Shaker is Moritz Glik's signature design, renowned for its dynamic treatment of gemstones. This design features white sapphire window panes that house shimmering fragments of gems, creating a lively and captivating visual effect as the stones move and dance with the wearer's movements.

How Did Moritz Glik's Begin Designing Jewelry?

Moritz Glik started his creative journey in Belo Horizonte, Brazil, originally in shoe design. After moving to New York in 1991, he discovered his passion for jewelry and a fascination with gemstones and precious metals. His global travels and experience as a master bench jeweler have significantly shaped his expertise and aesthetic vision.

What Inspires Moritz Glik's Jewelry Designs?

Moritz Glik is deeply inspired by classical architecture and fine art, blending traditional techniques with a playful, avant-garde sensibility. His New York-based designs are a reflection of his energetic pursuit of creative singularity, making each piece unique and distinct.
