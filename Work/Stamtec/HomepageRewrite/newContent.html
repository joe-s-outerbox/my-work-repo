High-Quality Metal Stamping Presses for Every Process

We recognize that every manufacturing operation has its own specific needs and challenges.We have spent more than 70 years as an industrial and automotive stamping press manufacturer, developing production line solutions for a variety of industries and manufacturing techniques. Our current lineup of products includes mechanical presses, forging presses, servo presses, and auxiliary equipment.

Mechanical Presses

Built using finite element analysis, our mechanical presses offer superior strength and maximum resistance in order to eliminate deflection. We stock tonnage ranges from 25 to 600, but can build custom mechanical presses with tonnages up to 3,000 if required by your production line.

Forging Presses

Available in the 220 to 4,000 tonnage range, our forging presses are used by an array of industries and metal fabrication companies. Forging presses offer a superior solution for the manufacture of complex and intricate parts that require maximum durability.

Servo Presses

Utilizing Stamtec's servo motor technology, our servo presses use high-torque, low-RPM servo motors and proprietary press controls to achieve an exceptional variety of stroke lengths and slide movement profiles while still offering full working energy at low speeds.

Auxiliary Equipment

From coil feeding equipment to quick die change systems, Stamtec offers a full range of auxiliary equipment that help you streamline your process and create efficiencies in your manufacturing chain. We produce everything, including press controls, monitoring tools, transfer systems, and press mounts.
