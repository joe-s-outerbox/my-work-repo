Can You Stain the Inside of a Log Home?

Yes, you can stain the inside of a log home. Interior stains for wood can help protect your logs and prevent rotting that can occur over time.

What Is the Best Finish for Interior Logs?

The best finish for interior logs is one developed explicitly for log cabins. It should repel water, resist stains, and allow interior log moisture vapor to escape.

Is It Better to Paint or Stain a Log Home?

Staining is often preferred for log homes because it allows the logs to breathe. Painting, on the other hand, seals the wood, trapping moisture inside and promoting rot. Stain also enhances the natural beauty of your logs.
