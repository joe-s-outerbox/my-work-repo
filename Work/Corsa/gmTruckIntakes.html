Frequently Asked Questions: Cold Air Intake Systems

Still have questions or want to learn more about cold air intake systems? Check out the answers to our most frequently asked questions below for more information.

What Does a Cold Air Intake System Do?

A cold air intake system replaces your vehicle's standard airbox with a more direct, larger-diameter intake tube. This allows cooler, denser air to enter the engine, improving combustion efficiency and performance gains.

Do I Need to Tune My Engine After Installing a Cold Air Intake?

CORSA cold air intake kits do not require tuning, but it's recommended for maximizing performance gains. A tune-up will help your engine's computer 'learn' the increased airflow from the new air intake, optimizing performance and efficiency.

Will Installing a Cold Air Intake Void My Vehicle's Warranty?

Generally, an aftermarket cold air intake won't void your entire vehicle warranty. However, if a problem directly linked to the intake system occurs, the specific repair might not be covered under warranty. Checking your warranty terms and consulting your dealer is always a good idea.
