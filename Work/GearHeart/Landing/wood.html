Can I Use Gearheart Industry Branding Irons for Branded Corporate Gifts Made From Different Materials?

We offer custom branding irons for wood in aluminum and brass. Aluminum branding plates are perfect for occasional use, offering a budget-friendly option; however, they can be more susceptible to damage at high temperatures if not heated correctly. On the other hand, brass plates are the go-to choice for professional settings. They boast superior durability and withstand harsh conditions, making them ideal for branding rough, painted, or dirty surfaces. Both materials deliver the same level of sharpness and detail in your branding.
