Requirements for Aerospace Actuators

With its complex challenges and high stakes, the aerospace sector demands equipment that adheres to the highest standard. Aerospace actuators must meet the following to be deemed fit:

  1. Reliability: Actuators in aerospace must perform consistently and predictably, where even a minute malfunction can lead to significant consequences. Durability and operational consistency are paramount.
  2. Weight Considerations: In the aerospace industry, weight is more than just a number; it's a determinant of efficiency, fuel consumption and overall performance. Aerospace actuators must strike a delicate balance, being lightweight without sacrificing strength or functionality.
  3. Precision & Accuracy: With work to be done in countless industries, actuators' movements must be precise and repeatable. Even slight deviations can impact performance, safety and outcomes.

We understand these factors are non-negotiable. Every aerospace actuator we design, manufacture or customize undergoes testing to ensure it meets our specifications for quality and functionality.
