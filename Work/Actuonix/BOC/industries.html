What Are Linear Actuators?

Linear actuators are devices that convert energy – usually electrical – into linear motion, enabling precise movement and control in various applications across multiple industries.

What Is the Lifespan of an Actuator?

We don't specify a lifespan for our actuators, as it varies widely depending on several factors such as load, side load, duty cycle, temperature, stall conditions, humidity and environmental particulates. Given the many applications our products are used for, it's challenging to determine a standard lifespan. We advise customers to conduct their own testing to assess the lifespan of the actuator in their specific application. You may find this article on extending the life of your actuator helpful.
