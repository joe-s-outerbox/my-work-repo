What Is A Medical Linear Actuator?

A medical linear actuator is a specialized device that creates motion in a straight line, as opposed to the rotational motion provided by a traditional motor. In the context of the healthcare industry, these actuators are integral for ensuring the precision and reliability of various medical applications, such as prosthetic limbs and medical testing devices.
