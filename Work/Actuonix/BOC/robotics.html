What Are Robotics Actuators Used For?

Robotics actuators play a crucial role in converting energy into motion. They function as the "muscles" of a robot, enabling it to move, lift or manipulate objects. Whether it's the fingers in a prosthetic hand or controlling secondary functions on a drone, actuators make these actions possible.

How Are Actuators Controlled in Robotic Systems?

Actuators can be controlled in a variety of ways depending on the application. Some common means of control include simple DPDT switches, RC servo, arduino, PWM, voltage, current as well as proprietary control systems.

What Factors Do I Need to Consider When Choosing an Actuator for My Robotics Project?

Selecting the right robot actuator depends on the specific needs of your robot, such as the required force, speed, range of motion and precision. Consider factors like the robot's weight, the tasks it will perform and its operating environment. Please get in touch if you need additional guidance.
