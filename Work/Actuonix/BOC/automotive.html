What Are Automotive Actuators?

Automotive actuators are devices that convert energy – typically electrical energy – into mechanical motion. This enables various functions in a vehicle.

Why Are Actuators Important in Modern Vehicles?

Actuators play a pivotal role in enhancing the comfort, safety and performance of modern vehicles. They help automate a wide range of functions for both OEMs and automotive enthusiasts.
