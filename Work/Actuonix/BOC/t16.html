Find the Right T-16 For Your Project

Actuonix offers three distinct T16 linear track actuator models: the T16 P-Series, S-Series and R-Series, each tailored for specific applications and control requirements.

T16 P-Series

T16 P-series linear track actuators stand out for their versatility and adaptability in control options. While these actuators do not come with an integrated controller, they are equipped with a feature that provides an analog position feedback signal. This signal can be integrated with an external controller for precise motion control. The distinct advantage of the "P" series lies in its compatibility with the Linear Actuator Control Board (LAC). This compatibility offers various control methods, including USB, through our intuitive Configuration Utility Software, 0-5V, 4-20mA, RC Servo or PWM.

Furthermore, the LAC board enhances the functionality of T16 P-series actuators by allowing on-board adjustments. Users can easily modify settings such as speed, end limits and sensitivity, tailoring performance to suit specific project requirements.

T16 S-Series

T16 S-series linear track actuators are engineered for straightforward and efficient operation. Motion back and forth is achieved simply by switching the polarity on the actuator's two leads.

A key safety feature of this series is the integration of end-of-stroke limit switches. These switches are designed to automatically shut down the unit if it reaches its end stop, thereby preventing damage or overextension. The versatility of the T16 S-series is further enhanced by its compatibility with various control methods. T16 S-series actuators can be operated using a DPDT switch, a relay or a brushed DC motor controller, offering flexibility in integrating different control systems.

T16 R-Series

Enhance the functionality of your T16-P actuator by adding an Ext-R adapter, transforming it into an RC-compatible unit. This convenient adapter package comes complete with an RC extension cable, enabling effortless plug-and-play connection directly to your receiver. It's designed for simplicity and ease of use, ensuring that each T16-P actuator can be quickly and efficiently adapted for RC operation. This addition opens up new possibilities for integrating T16-P actuators into a broader range of remote-controlled applications.

Note: One Ext-R adapter is necessary for each actuator to achieve compatibility.

A Deeper Look at T16 Linear Track Actuators

Our T16 linear track actuators come in three stroke lengths: 100mm (3.9"), 200mm (7.9") and 300mm (11.8"). Additionally, they are available in three gearing options: 22:1, 64:1 or 256:1. This versatility enables T16 series track actuators to cater to a wide array of applications, ensuring optimal performance in various settings.

The 256:1 gearing configuration allows the T16 mini track actuator to exert a remarkable maximum force of 67 lbs., demonstrating its power in compact form. Conversely, with the 22:1 gearing option, the actuator achieves a top speed of 46mm/s (1.8 "/s), balancing force with agility.
