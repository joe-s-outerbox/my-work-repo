Automotive actuators can be found throughout modern cars, RVs and commercial vehicles. They drive some of the basic functions that you enjoy such as opening and closing vent doors, opening and closing windows and sliding doors, engine control and much more. Automotive linear actuators range from small to large and run behind the scenes controlling key functions to make our driving experience easier and more comfortable.

Linear actuators are also ideal for use in aftermarket applications. Some people find that they want to add some automation to custom vehicles, hot rods and classics as well as motorcycles. For years, people wishing to customize their cars had limited options to work with. Motors with complicated drive systems, weak rotary servos or super expensive micro actuators.
