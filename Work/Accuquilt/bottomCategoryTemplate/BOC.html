Frequently Asked Questions About Embroidery Designs

Here are answers to some common questions we've received about embroidery designs. Please reach out to our customer experience team if you have other questions.

What Should I Look for When Exploring Embroidery Machine Patterns?

When seeking embroidery machine patterns, it's important to consider the quality of the design, the size and stitch count, and the compatibility with your machine. Additionally, you may want to look for embroidery quilt designs that include variations or come with detailed instructions or video tutorials.

  • Animals
  • Shapes
  • Letters & Numbers
  • Holiday/Seasonal
  • Flowers
  • Feathers
  • Stars

What File Formats Are Compatible With Embroidery Machines?

The most common file formats that work with embroidery machines are DST, EXP, HUS, JEF, PES, VIP and VP3. It's always important to check your machine's manual to ensure compatibility.
