About

Phone Number Inclusion

Trinity built out an A/B test within our partner’s automated email marketing journey with the goal of increasing call volume for a free consultation. The test included a unique phone number in the header to not only track clicks on the phone number, but also track all direct calls.

Following completion of the test, Trinity tracked results in Google Data Studio and calculated the revenue gains based on the phone call goal value.
