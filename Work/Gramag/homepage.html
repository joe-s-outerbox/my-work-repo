What is the difference between air ride seats and standard semi-truck seats?

Air ride seats use air suspension technology to absorb road vibrations, providing a smoother ride and reducing driver fatigue. Standard seats do not have this suspension, so air ride seats are often preferred by drivers who spend long hours on the road.
