Contain Hazardous Liquids with Containment Pads

The Containment Pad™ is a heavy-duty, drive-on containment system designed to contain incidental fuel and hazardous material spills. Polystar’s secondary containment system can stand alone or be coupled with additional containment pads. Containment pads create larger configurations for an entire secondary fuel containment system. Count on Polystar’s Containment Pad for vehicle fuel and hazardous material spill containment.

Containment Pads Fuel Containment System Construction and Capabilities

Corrosion resistant fiberglass encapsulates each of our steel vehicle containment pads. Our quarter-inch-thick steel plates are coated in a nominal 1/16″ fiberglass lamination. And for U.V. protection, our containment pads include a gel-coat surface resin of equal chemical resistance. Polystar’s low-profile fuel and hazardous liquid containment pads provide a crack-free surface. Both large tanker trucks or low clearance passenger cars and fuel tankers use Polystar Containment Pads for oil tank containment of liquid and hazardous materials spills. Our Containment Pad tri-linear side walls prevent damage when driven over by heavy vehicles. An angular, composite, steel-cored stealth shape forms the tri-linear side walls, making this fuel containment system durable and reliable. Get the toughest secondary containment solution for truck spillage available today by using our spill containment system, the Containment Pad.

Benefits of the Containment Pads

Polystar’s Containment Pad systems are ideal for fuel, hazardous liquid, and vehicle containment. Specifically, liquid terminals, transloading facilities, service stations, and fill stands greatly benefit from spill containment pads where a wide variety of vehicles might use the system. Additionally, containment pads safely capture incidental spills and drips from generators, hydraulic mules, or other pieces of messy equipment. As a surface-mounted vehicle containment pad, the Containment Pad does not crack like concrete systems. Modular in all sizes, our fuel spill containment systems work for a variety of applications. Need to manage oil and grease when washing heavy-duty vehicles? The Containment Pad can be used as a mobile water containment or water reclamation pad to make your heavy-duty vehicle clean-up quick and easy while mitigating potential risks to the environment.

Vehicle Spill Containment Pads Additions & Options

One or more threaded drain ports equip each of our vehicle Containment Pads. Ball valves or passive shutoff filters on these ports allow rainwater to drain, but close the port if hydrocarbons are released. Floor drains may also be installed if desired.
