Vehicle Spill Containment Pads Additions & Options

One or more threaded drain ports equip each of our spill containment pads. Ball valves or passive shutoff filters on these ports allow rainwater to drain, but close the port if hydrocarbons are released. Floor drains may also be installed if desired.
