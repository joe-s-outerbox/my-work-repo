Site Preparation for Oilfield Pads

The substrate must be able to support the weight of any Polystar system plus the weight of a loaded vehicle with no more than 1/2“ deflection at the axle. The customer is responsible for site preparation. Recommended final site prep includes limestone screenings compacted on 3″ limestone rock base.

Equipment and Labor Requirements for Oilfield Pad Setup

  • Crane with operator and four way chain spreader to offload trucks and place pans in place
  • Roustabout labor (four men) hooking up from truck to site and connecting bulkhead fittings joining pans into one large containment area
  • Impact wrenches to tighten bulk head fittings & spud bars to maneuver pads
