What Is Operation Clean Sweep?

Operation Clean Sweep (OCS) is an industry-led initiative aimed at preventing the loss of plastic resins — pellets, flakes and powders — during handling by the various entities involved in the plastics industry. Founded by the Plastics Industry Association in 1991 and managed in partnership with the American Chemistry Council's (ACC) Plastics Division, OCS underscores the plastics industry's commitment to achieving zero plastic resin loss in operations. The program operates globally, with participation from companies in over 60 countries, encompassing the entire plastics value chain from manufacturers and distributors of plastic materials to transportation companies and processors​​.

The essence of OCS is to foster a collaborative effort among all stakeholders in the plastics industry to minimize and ultimately eliminate plastic waste in the environment. This initiative aims not only to protect water bodies and wildlife from pollution but also to enhance worker safety and promote a circular economy by keeping plastics within the economic cycle rather than letting them become environmental waste​​.

How Can Companies Participate in Operation Clean Sweep?

Companies can become part of Operation Clean Sweep by pledging to adhere to the program's best practices for spill prevention and plastic containment. This involves implementing processes and systems to ensure materials are managed responsibly throughout the production cycle, minimizing the risk of plastic materials entering the environment.

How Does Operation Clean Sweep Align With Global Environmental Protection Efforts?

Operation Clean Sweep is a vital part of global environmental protection, focusing on the reduction of plastic waste and the promotion of sustainable industry practices. By aiming to eliminate plastic resin loss, the initiative directly contributes to lessening plastic pollution, thereby supporting the broader goals of conserving biodiversity and maintaining ecological balance.

What Are the Long-Term Benefits of Participating in Operation Clean Sweep for Companies and the Environment?

For companies, participation in Operation Clean Sweep demonstrates a commitment to environmental responsibility and leads to operational efficiencies and cost savings by minimizing waste. Environmentally, the benefits are profound, including reduced pollution, healthier ecosystems and the conservation of resources. Such efforts collectively support the global movement towards sustainability and the preservation of water quality.

Can Small Businesses Also Contribute to Operation Clean Sweep?

Absolutely. Small businesses play a crucial role in Operation Clean Sweep by adopting spill-prevention techniques and containment measures within their operations. Even modest efforts can have a significant impact when collectively applied across the industry, demonstrating that size does not limit a company's ability to contribute positively to environmental protection.

How is Water Conservation Linked to Operation Clean Sweep?

Water conservation and Operation Clean Sweep are intrinsically linked through the common goal of minimizing environmental impact. By preventing plastic spills, the initiative helps protect water bodies from pollution, thus contributing to the broader objective of conserving water resources and maintaining water quality control for ecosystems and human use alike.
