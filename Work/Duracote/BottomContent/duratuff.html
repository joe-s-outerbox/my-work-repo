heading DURA-TUFF® - Durable & Anti Static Vinyl Fabric left column

Dura-Tuff flexible vinyl fabric materials are expertly engineered for protective cover applications, including mattress ticking, gurney mattress covers, clean room curtains, shelving and tray liners, chair underlays, and more. This durable, anti-static fabric is ideal for both sewn and heat-sealed fabrication, meeting all specifications for manufacturing, installation, and end-use performance.

Designed with a black conductive front and a static-dissipative colored back, Dura-Tuff offers dimensionally stable, flame-resistant, fluid-proof, non-tacky, anti-static, and antimicrobial properties. This tough vinyl fabric material also resists dirt, stains, mildew, and fungal contamination, making it an excellent choice for high-performance needs.

As a leading choice among vinyl fabric and static dissipative material manufacturers, Duracote’s Dura-Tuff and Dura Pro vinyl fabric can be customized to match color and specification requirements for demanding environments. Whether you need vinyl lining fabric for clean rooms, healthcare, or industrial settings, Dura-Tuff provides a protective, easy-to-clean surface, enhancing longevity and performance across a range of uses.
