FAQs About Soap Dispensers for Vacation Rentals

At the Polished Jar, we aim to make shopping for soap dispensers for Airbnbs as simple as possible. That includes answering your questions when they come up. Here are some answers to our most frequently asked questions. Still have questions? Contact us today.

What is the easiest way to refill reusable soap dispensers without making a mess?

Refilling can be somewhat challenging when you use thick shampoo and conditioner. The simplest solution is to use a washable and flexible funnel. A standard plastic or metal funnel keeps thin hand and dish soaps properly directed into the container during pouring.

How do I choose soap dispensers for my rentals?

Start by finding soap dispensers that match your rental property’s overall design. We advise people to look for high-quality materials and designs that are simple to clean and maintain. Durability is also a factor since vacation rental items can take a lot of abuse.

Do I need to perform any maintenance on refillable soap dispensers?

Most of our containers are very low maintenance. We recommend washing them by hand instead of using the dishwasher. Simply wiping the outside clean is usually enough to keep them looking great. You can use a toothpick or pin to remove any buildup and rinse with warm water if you need to clean the pumps.
