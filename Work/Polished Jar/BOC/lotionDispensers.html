FAQs: Custom Lotion Bottles

Below, we've addressed some common questions about our custom lotion bottles. Please get in touch if you'd like additional information about any of our products.

Can I refill my dispenser with any type of soap or lotion?

Our dispensers are designed to be refillable, supporting sustainable practices. They work well with a wide range of liquid soaps and lotions. The exception is soaps that are high in lye, chlorine, or essential oils. .

How do I clean my lotion dispenser?

To keep your plastic or glass lotion dispenser looking its best, simply wipe it down with a damp cloth. For a deeper clean, use mild soap and water. Avoid harsh chemicals to preserve the finish.

Are the custom design options waterproof?

Absolutely! All custom designs are waterproof to ensure that your personalization remains pristine even with regular use.

What sizes are available for the lotion dispensers?

Our dispensers are available in several sizes to accommodate different space needs, from compact models like our 8oz bottles to larger options like our 16oz and 32oz options.
