They're the preferred method for illuminating outdoor parking areas because they offer many advantages over old-style lighting fixtures. Key features include:

  • Energy Efficiency: LED lighting uses significantly less energy than traditional light fixtures, resulting in lower operating costs and few carbon emissions.
  • Lasting Performance: LEDs burn longer, so they don't need to be replaced as often. This is a major benefit, given the labor involved in replacing parking lot lights.
