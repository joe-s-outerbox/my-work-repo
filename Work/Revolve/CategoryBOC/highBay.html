Types of High Bay LED Lighting

High bay LED lighting is essential for illuminating large indoor spaces with high ceilings, typically ranging from 15 to 40 feet. These lights provide bright, uniform illumination in warehouses, gymnasiums, factories, and other industrial or commercial spaces. There are several types of high bay LED lighting, each designed to meet specific needs:

Round (UFO) High Bay LED Lights

Round or UFO high bay lights are known for their compact, powerful design. Their circular shape enables wide light distribution, making them ideal for spaces that require broad, uniform coverage. These fixtures are easy to install and maintain, offering high energy efficiency and long lifespans.

Contractors highly favor our round high bay LED light fixtures for new construction projects due to their unmatched efficiency and maintenance-free performance. With an aluminum die-cast mold for superior heat dissipation and a modern design, round high bay lights not only enhance performance but also elevate the aesthetic of large industrial spaces.

Linear High Bay LED Lights

Linear high bay lights are designed to resemble traditional fluorescent tube fixtures but offer significantly greater energy efficiency. They are ideal for illuminating aisles and long spaces such as supermarkets, storage facilities, and industrial areas like warehouses, gymnasiums, and factories. With their sleek, rectangular shape, linear high bays have long been a go-to solution for large-scale lighting.

If you're upgrading from fluorescent fixtures, linear high bay LED lights are a cost-effective option. These fixtures are easy to install, often suspended by chains, though they can also be mounted directly to the ceiling.

You'll find several styles of industrial high bay LED lights, but they typically fall into two categories: round or linear high bay LED light fixtures. Both types will serve you well, providing bright, even illumination that enhances visibility and safety in commercial and industrial settings. It comes down to aesthetics and personal preference.

Why LED High Bay Lights Are Best

It's no secret that LED lamps are your best choice for every type of lighting, from commercial high bay LED lights to outdoor stadium lights and residential lighting solutions. Advantages of LED high bay fixtures include:

  • More Energy Efficient: High bay LED lights get top marks for energy efficiency because they consume far less energy than metal halide, high-pressure sodium, or fluorescent lights. That translates to lower energy bills for you and a lower carbon footprint for the environment
  • Longer Lasting: LED lights burn bright and far longer than old-style bulbs. An LED lamp may last up to 50,000 hours or even longer. That translates to extra savings on maintenance costs.
  • Improved Brightness: Industrial high bay LED lights can provide higher brightness levels than traditional lighting sources. With fewer fixtures, you can achieve better lighting coverage, reducing your overall lighting expense.
  • Better Color Rendering: High bay LED lights render better color than traditional lighting sources. Colors appear more vivid and accurate, which is important in many industries, such as manufacturing, warehousing, and retail.
  • Increased Safety: LED lights produce less heat than traditional lighting sources, which may reduce the risk of fires and other hazards. Additionally, they don't contain harmful chemicals such as mercury, making them safer for the environment.
  • More Sustainable: LED lights are made from easily recyclable materials and designed to reduce waste. Additionally, they use less energy, reducing greenhouse gas emissions and the overall environmental impact.

What's the Difference Between LED High Bay Fixtures and Low Bay Lighting?

LED high bay fixtures are designed for industrial spaces with ceilings that are 20 feet or higher. Low bay LED lights are engineered for ceiling heights of 15 feet or less.

How Many High Bay LED Light Fixtures Do I Need?

To calculate the number of high bay LED light fixtures needed, begin by measuring the room's height and width to determine the total square footage. Next, consider the desired lighting level, measured in foot candles, which is the amount of light that falls on a surface. This will vary depending on the task being performed in the space. Once you have these figures, you can consult a lighting professional or contact us to determine the number of high bay LED lights needed to light your space adequately.

Who Uses Commercial High Bay LED Lights?

Commercial high bay LED lights are used by various businesses and industries that require bright and efficient lighting in large indoor spaces with high ceilings. Examples include:

  • Warehouses
  • Manufacturing Facilities
  • Retail Stores
  • Gymnasiums
  • Sports Arenas
  • Convention Centers

Can High Bay LED Lights Be Dimmed?

Yes, many high bay LED lights can be dimmed, allowing you to adjust the lighting levels to meet your specific needs. However, not all high bays are dimmable, so it's best to check the product specifications before making a purchase.
