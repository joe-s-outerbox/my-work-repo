Outdoor LED Lighting Solutions for Your Property

Commercial buildings and their environs must be adequately lit to ensure the safety and security of people and property. These outdoor LED lights help you accomplish these lighting setups:

  • Wall Packs: Light fixtures mounted on walls to provide safety and security while enhancing your building's appearance to attract tenants, clients and shoppers.
  • Flood Lights: Flood lights provide a wide beam of illumination for sports fields, parking lots and construction sites.
  • Area/Shoebox Lights: Area or shoebox lights are light fixtures that provide illumination over parking lots, residential neighborhoods, corporate campuses and other outdoor spaces.
  • Dusk-to-Dawn Lights: These lights have a photocell that automatically turns your lights on at dusk and turns them off again when the sun comes up. Dusk-to-dawn lights are ideal for driveways, sheds, garages, roads and docks.
  • Canopy Lights: This type of LED outdoor lighting fixture is installed under building overhangs or other covered areas such as gas stations, soffits, parking garages and carports.
  • Bollards: These outdoor security lights are set in posts that are placed along pathways, gardens and other outdoor areas.
  • Vapor-Tight Lights: Vapor-tight lights are heavy-duty, weather-resistant light fixtures designed to withstand harsh outdoor conditions.

What's the Difference Between LED Garage Strip Lights and Vapor-Tight Light Fixtures?

LED garage strip lights are typically long, narrow strips of LED lights designed to provide bright and even lighting across a wide area. In contrast, vapor-tight light fixtures are heavier-duty and designed to withstand harsher conditions. While both types of lighting can be effective in a garage setting, vapor-tight lighting may be more appropriate for garages that require fixtures with higher levels of durability.

What Are the Benefits of Using Outdoor LED Lighting?

Outdoor LED lighting has several benefits, including energy efficiency, long lifespan, low maintenance, and versatile designs. Outdoor LED lights take less energy than traditional incandescent bulbs, saving you money on energy bills. They also enjoy a longer lifespan and require less frequent replacements and maintenance.

How Do I Choose the Right Commercial Outdoor Lighting for My Needs?

When choosing commercial outdoor lighting, focus on the purpose of the lighting and the size of the area you want to illuminate. You should also consider the style and design of the fixture to ensure it complements your outdoor space. Wattage is also a consideration. The best wattage for outdoor LED lighting depends on the size of the area you're illuminating. The following shows the available wattages for each of the major types of LED commercial outdoor lighting:

  • Wall Packs: 15 to 135 watts
  • Flood Lights: 15 to 850 watts
  • Area Lights: 40 to 310 watts
  • Canopy Lights: 27 to 90 watts
  • Dusk-to-Dawn: 27 to 150 watts
  • Bollards: 26 watts

What Is the Average Lifespan of Industrial Outdoor LED Lighting?

Industrial outdoor LED lighting has a longer lifespan than traditional incandescent bulbs – an average lifespan of 25,000 to 50,000 hours. Outdoor LED lights may last up to 25 times longer than conventional bulbs, reducing the need for frequent replacements and saving you money on maintenance costs.

Can Outdoor LED Lighting Fixtures Be Used in Wet Conditions?

Most outdoor LED lighting fixtures are designed to be weather-resistant and can be used in wet conditions. Look for fixtures with an IP rating of at least 65 or higher, which indicates the light is protected from dust and water.
