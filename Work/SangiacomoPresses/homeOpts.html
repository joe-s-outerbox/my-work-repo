Benefits of Shortest Stroke in Stamping

Nearly all metal stamping operations can benefit from utilizing the shortest stroke length possible. This is why the adjustable stroke stamping press delivered by Sangaicomo is equipped with our adjustable stroke technology. This allows users to optimize their production by using the shortest stroke possible for a given forming application. When the adjustable stroke feature is used to achieve the shortest possible stroke, it can provide many benefits for your metal forming operation across a wide variety of applications. Some of these benefits include enhanced part quality, improved die life, and extended press life.

The Key Advantages of Adjustable Strokes

  • Streamlined operations and enhanced adaptability due to a multi-functional press capable of stamping, blanking, coining, drawing, and bending.
  • Boosted productivity with the advantage of shorter, more efficient strokes.
  • Minimized downtime and decreased maintenance expenses.
  • Extended tool durability by up to 50%.
  • Prolonged lifespan of the press.
  • Cost-efficient solutions, ensuring profitability even for short-run production tasks.
  • Superior quality in stamped parts produced.
