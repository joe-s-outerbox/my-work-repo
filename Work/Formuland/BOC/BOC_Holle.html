How do you prepare Holle formula?

Our formula comes in different sizes - 400g, 600g, and 800g - to make up to 100 fl oz, 150 fl oz, and 200 fl oz of formula respectively. No need to worry about running out!

Simple steps for preparation:

  • Step 1: Sterilize the bottle, nipple, and ring in boiling water for 3 minutes before each use.
  • Step 2: Boil water and cool it down to approximately 120 °F. Pour half into the bottle.
  • Step 3: Fill the measuring spoon with the indicated quantity of milk powder and add it to the bottle.
  • Step 4: Shake thoroughly, add the remaining water, shake again, and cool it down to a safe drinking temperature of around 100 °F.
