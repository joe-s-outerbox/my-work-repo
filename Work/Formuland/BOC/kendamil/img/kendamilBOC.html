How do you prepare Kendamil formula?

Experience the simplicity and quality of Kendamil baby formula preparation.

How many days after opening the Kendamil Formula is it good for?

  • After opening, use the product within 4 weeks (28 days).
  • Store opened and unopened containers in a clean, cool, and dry place, away from heat sources and direct sunlight.
  • Close the lid tightly when not in use to prevent contamination.
  • Do not refrigerate the formula, as it can clump in humid conditions.
  • Also, avoid freezing powdered baby formula.
