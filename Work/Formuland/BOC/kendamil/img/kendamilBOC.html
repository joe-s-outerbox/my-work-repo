Kendamil Formula FAQs

Is Kendamil formula suitable for babies with digestive sensitivities?

Kendamil goat milk-based formula is a suitable alternative option for infants allergic to cow milk formulas as it contains natural prebiotics and has anti-infection properties that help solve digestive issues. As always, consult with your pediatrician to determine the best formula for your baby

Can I switch between different stages of Kendamil formula?

Yes, Kendamil formula stages are made to cater to the changing nutritional needs of babies. However, it is always best to consult with a pediatrician before making any changes to your baby's feeding routine.

Where is Kendamil formula manufactured?

Kendamil formula is manufactured in Kendal, Cumbria, in the United Kingdom. As a British family owned business Kendamil has 60 years of experience in producing high quality baby milk.

Are Kendamil formulas available in the USA?

Yes, Kendamil formula is available in the USA and is committed to providing high-quality and sustainable formula options to parents in the US.
