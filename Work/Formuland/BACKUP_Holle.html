How Do I Choose the Right Holle Formula for My Baby?

Choosing the right Holle Organic Baby Formula for your little one is easy. Simply choose the stage that corresponds to your baby's age and needs.

Holle Formula Stages

Holle formula is available in several stages, each designed to meet the specific nutritional needs of babies at different stages of development. All products are organic and contain Omega3 (ALA), Omega3 (DHA) and Omega 6 to support healthy growth and development in all stages.

  • Holle Stage Pre is perfect for newborns, while Holle Stage 1 is ideal for babies from birth to 6 months.
  • Holle Stage 2 is great for babies from 6 to 10 months, and Holle Stage 3 is perfect for babies from 10 months to 1 year.
  • Holle Stage 4 is designed for toddlers over 1 year of age.
