1. Catering to Fragrance Sensitivity

When planning an event, the last thing you want is a guest sneezing or rubbing their eyes because of a scented candle. While a bit of fragrance might seem harmless, many people are more sensitive to scents than you'd expect. In large gatherings, it's hard to predict who might have allergies, asthma, or other sensitivities that can be triggered by strong aromas.

That's where candles for fragrance sensitivity enter into the picture, ensuring no guest feels uncomfortable or distracted by lingering scents. They light up the space without adding anything extra to the air, making them ideal for events with a diverse guest list.

Plus, venues are increasingly leaning toward odorless candles to meet client demands for allergy-friendly settings. Whether it's a corporate event, wedding, or charity gala, no one has to worry about overpowering smells clashing with personal sensitivities.
