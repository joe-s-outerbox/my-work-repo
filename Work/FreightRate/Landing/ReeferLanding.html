What Are the Typical Specs For Refrigerated Trailers?

For most refrigerated trailers, the maximum freight weight possible is 42,000 to 45,0000 pounds (depending on the truck). The maximum length on most loads inside the main deck is 48'-0" to 53'-0" (again, depending on the truck).

The maximum width on reefer trailers is 8'-2", with the height maxing out at 8'-0". But, if you don't need much room for your shipment, just opt for LTL reefer trucking services. With the large number of freight carriers that Freight Rate Central works with, you can find refrigerated trailers of any size.

Can Reefer Trailers Keep My Goods From Freezing?

Although reefer trailers are primarily used for transporting reefer loads that require refrigeration, these trailers can also be heated to prevent products from freezing during the winter season. Whether it's cooling or heating, reefer trailers offer a reliable solution for transporting a wide range of goods.
