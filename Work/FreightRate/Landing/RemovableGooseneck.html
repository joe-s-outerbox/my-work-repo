Removable Gooseneck Trailers: Typical Variations

Freight Rate Centrals understands the ins and outs of shipping oversized RGN loads. That's why we offer a wide range of removable gooseneck trailers to meet your exact shipping requirements.

When it comes to removable gooseneck trailers, there are three essential components: the gooseneck, which connects the trailer to the truck cab, the drop deck and the step back up over the rear axles of the trailer. Removable gooseneck trailers have a detachable front that can be dropped to the ground, providing a ramp for easy loading of oversized freight. This unique feature also means that RGN trailers drop in the middle, providing extra height for your cargo. This can be a game-changer in complying with the rules and regulations associated with heavy equipment transport in different countries, provinces or states.

Moreover, RGN transport offers an extended trailer option where the drop-deck area can be extended up to 61ft, increasing the maximum RGN trailer length to 74ft. This allows for much larger usable space, making RGN trucking an excellent choice for transporting oversized equipment. Frequently Asked Questions: Removable Gooseneck Trailers

If you have any questions or need more information about removable gooseneck trailers, check out the answers to our most frequently asked questions below.

What Is the Difference Between RGN Trailers and Lowboys?

When transporting heavy equipment and oversized products, you have a few options, including lowboy and removable gooseneck trailers. While both are great for hauling, one may be more suitable for your specific needs. For example, if you prefer to drive your shipment directly onto the trailer, RGN trailers would be your best bet. However, if you're looking for a more cost-effective option, a lowboy might be better, as RGN trailers can be a bit more expensive.

What Is the Maximum Weight for RGN Trailers?

While the maximum freight weight for RGN loads is 42,000 pounds, these trailers can have over 20 axles, enabling them to carry up to a whopping 150,000 pounds of cargo.

What Are the Maximum Freight Dimensions for RGN Trailers?

Removable gooseneck trailers are built to accommodate a wide variety of freight dimensions. The maximum length of the RGN well on the main deck is 29 feet, and the maximum width is 8.5 feet. Additionally, our RGN trailers can legally transport cargo with a maximum height of 11.6 feet.
