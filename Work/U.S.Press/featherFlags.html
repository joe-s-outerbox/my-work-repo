Common Uses For Printed Feather Flags

Feather flags are versatile marketing tools commonly used in various settings. They are unique because they are narrow and tall, reaching above the crowd to maximize their visibility. Here are some of the main ways that different industries use printed feather flags:

  • Retail Promotions: Feather flag signs are perfect for retail environments, drawing attention to special sales, new product launches, or seasonal promotions. Placed near entrances or along busy sidewalks, they help attract foot traffic and entice customers into your store.
  • Trade Shows and Events: At trade shows, conventions, and fairs, custom feather flags with poles are indispensable for standing out among the crowd. Their tall and eye-catching design makes it easy for attendees to find your booth, increasing visibility and foot traffic.
  • Real Estate: Real estate agents use advertising feather flags to highlight open houses, new listings, or rental properties. Placed strategically near properties, they guide potential buyers and renters directly to the location.
  • Restaurants and Cafés: Feather flags for businesses in the food industry can announce daily specials, grand openings, or special events. Positioned outside the establishment, they help attract diners and create a vibrant atmosphere.
  • Sporting Events and Competitions: Printed feather flags are often used at sporting events to mark registration areas, start/finish lines, and sponsor zones. Their durability and visibility make them ideal for both indoor and outdoor events.
  • Festivals and Community Events: Feather flag banners enhance the festive atmosphere at community events, fairs, and parades. They can be used to indicate different sections, direct attendees, or promote event sponsors.
  • Corporate Advertising: Feather flags with printing of your corporate logo and messaging are excellent for increasing brand awareness. Use them at company events, conferences, and seminars to reinforce your brand presence.
  • Schools and Universities: Educational institutions use custom feather flags for open houses, campus tours, and events. They provide clear direction and information for visitors, creating an organized and welcoming environment.

Feather Flags FAQ

Custom feather flags are versatile and effective for improving visibility and directing traffic to your business or event. Below, we've compiled answers to common questions about our feather flag printing:

What's the difference between feather flags and tear drop flags?

Feather flags and tear drop flags are closely related: both products are printed on polyester material, displayed on a pole, and often serve the same purpose. The key difference between the two is the shape of the flag, which comes down to your personal preference. Note that the shape of the flag determines the overall size as well as pricing, so be sure to check both of these products to find the best fit for your needs.

Where are feather banners usually displayed?

Most commonly, feather flag banners are displayed outdoors close to a road in order to draw attention to your business, organization, or event. When displayed outdoors, we recommend using our ground stake if you plan on inserting it into soil, or our cross base with water weight bag to add additional anchorage to a sidewalk. A cross base alone is sufficient for indoor use, such as at a trade show.

Is the pole included with my feather flag purchase?

Yes, each custom printed feather flag kit includes an aluminum pole for display as well as a nylon bag for easy transportation.

Is the ground/base hardware included?

By default, the base attachments are not included with your feather flag. However, simply select the base attachment of your choice using our pricing calculator above, and it will add it to your order.
