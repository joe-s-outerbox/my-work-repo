Gastric sleeve revision is becoming a more commonly-performed procedure due to the fact that the sleeve has grown in popularity to the point where it is now the most common weight loss surgery in the United States.  It's a great operation-it's quick, simple, has a low complication rate, and yet has a lot of power.  However, with any weight loss surgery there are patients who just try their best and don't achieve their goals, or they don't get there because they don't put in the effort.  Either way it's nice to know that the sleeve is an easily-revisable procedure in most cases!  Sleeves are most commonly converted to either a SIPS or a gastric bypass, but in some cases can be revised endoscopically in a procedure that is very similar to an endoscopic sleeve gastroplasty.  Explore your options below and if you think a revision might be for you, request an appointment with one of our expert weight loss surgeons!
