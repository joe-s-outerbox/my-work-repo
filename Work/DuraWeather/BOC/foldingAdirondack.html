FAQs About Folding Adirondack Chairs

What is HDPE, and why is it used for these chairs?

HDPE, or high-density polyethylene, is a durable and weather-resistant recycled plastic. It is used for DuraWeather folding Adirondack chairs because it doesn't crack, splinter, or rot.

Can I leave my chair outside all year round?

Yes, DuraWeather foldable Adirondack chairs are designed to withstand all weather conditions. They’re UV-protected and fade-resistant, making them suitable for year-round outdoor use.

How do I clean my DuraWeather Adirondack chair?

Cleaning is simple. Use mild soap and water, and wipe down the chair with a soft cloth or sponge. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials.
