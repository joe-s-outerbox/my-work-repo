Frequently Asked Questions About Outdoor Dining Tables & Chairs

What makes poly outdoor dining sets different from other materials?

Poly outdoor dining sets stand out for their durability and low maintenance features. Crafted from high-quality poly lumber, they resist fading, cracking, and splintering, ensuring long-lasting beauty without extensive upkeep. Contact us for more information about how our poly sets differ from others.

Are Adirondack dining sets comfortable for extended seating?

Yes, Adirondack dining sets are designed with comfort in mind. The wide, sloping seats provide ample space, encouraging a relaxed and comfortable dining experience. They are perfect for enjoying leisurely meals and engaging in lively conversations.

What maintenance is required for poly outdoor dining sets?

Poly outdoor dining sets are virtually maintenance-free. They can be easily cleaned with soap and water, and you won't need to worry about staining, painting, or sealing. Enjoy the beauty of your outdoor furniture without the hassle of extensive upkeep.

Can I leave my Adirondack dining set outside all year round?

Adirondack dining sets are designed for outdoor use and can withstand different seasons. While they are built to be weather-resistant, using protective covers during extreme weather conditions can help prolong their lifespan.

Are there additional accessories available for outdoor dining sets??

Yes! Enhance your outdoor dining experience with our range of outdoor dining accessories. From seat cushions for added comfort to protective covers for inclement weather, we offer complimentary items to make your outdoor space even more inviting.
