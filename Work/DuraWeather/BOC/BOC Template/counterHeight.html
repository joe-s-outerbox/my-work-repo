What Is the Difference Between Counter and Bar Height Patio Dining Sets?

Counter-height outdoor table and chair sets stand at a comfortable height of about 34-36 inches, making them ideal for casual dining and socializing. Bar-height outdoor table and chair sets, on the other hand, typically measure around 40-42 inches high, providing a traditional bar-like experience. Both styles offer unique comfort and aesthetics to match your outdoor entertaining needs.

How Do I Maintain My Counter Height Adirondack Chairs and Other Poly Furniture?

Maintaining your HDPE chairs or bar-height patio dining set is easy. A simple cleaning routine with soap and water is all it takes to keep your set looking new. Thanks to the HDPE material, your bar-height outdoor table and chairs are built to resist stains, weather and UV rays, ensuring they maintain their good looks without extensive upkeep or painting.​

What Is a Tête-à-Tête Counter Height Outdoor Table and Chairs?

Our tête-à-tête counter height outdoor table and chairs set is a charming furniture arrangement designed for two people to engage in conversation.
