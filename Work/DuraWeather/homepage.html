Frequently Asked Questions About Poly Outdoor Furniture

Get quick answers to common questions about our poly lumber furniture and materials.

What is Poly Lumber?

Poly Lumber, or High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), is a durable material made from 100% recycled plastic, with DuraWeather Poly® comprising 95% recycled plastic enhanced with all-season additives for extra longevity.

How Does Poly Lumber Furniture Compare to Wood?

Unlike wood, poly deck furniture requires no annual maintenance like staining or sanding and is resistant to mold, rot and insects, making it a more durable and eco-friendly choice.

Where Is DuraWeather Poly Furniture Made?

We are proud to be an American company. All of our poly outdoor furniture and accessories are made in our U.S.-based plants in Nevada, Ohio and Wisconsin, using 95% Ultra-Premium HDPE and 5% UV inhibitors. This process ensures a high-quality, fade-resistant product while recycling approximately 210 million plastic bottles annually.
