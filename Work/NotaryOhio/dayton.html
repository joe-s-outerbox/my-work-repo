Dayton, Ohio Notary Course Length & Cost

The notary course in Dayton costs $130, payable before starting the required training. This fee does not include the cost of the mandatory background check, which screens for any outstanding warrants or felony convictions.

The notary course is conducted online and designed to fully prepare you for your responsibilities as a notary public in Ohio. The training takes three hours to complete. If you don't pass the exam on your first attempt, you can retake it after 30 days. Keep in mind that all fees are non-refundable

Next Steps for Dayton Notary Public Commission Application

Once you have successfully completed the notary course, the next step is to head to the Secretary of State's office to submit your "New Notary Application." This application pulls together all the essential components you've gathered throughout the process. It's the final step in the application procedure, and approval rests with the Secretary of State, who may decline the application if no notary positions are available.

What You'll Need

To complete your application, you must provide:

  • Your passing test score
  • Background check results
  • Full name
  • Residential address
  • Recent signature
  • $15 fee payable directly to the Secretary of State's office.

Review and Certification

The Secretary of State will review all submitted materials. Once everything is verified and in order, you will receive an officially stamped certificate. This certification confirms your status as a notary public in Dayton. At this point, you can contact the State Bar Association to obtain your official stamp and any necessary supplies.

Dayton Notary Renewal

To renew your notary public commission in Dayton, simply visit the Ohio Notary Services website and select the "Renew" option. This process allows you to renew either your traditional notary commission or your authorization as an online notary public directly within Dayton. It's a straightforward process designed to ensure you can continue your notarial duties without interruption.
