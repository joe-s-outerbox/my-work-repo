Responsibilities of a Notary Public

A notary public is a citizen with the authority to certify signatures on legal documents. By acting as an unbiased witness, a notary helps prevent fraudulent signatures on mortgages, wills, powers of attorney and other legal documents.

As a notary public in Dayton, Ohio, it's your job to ensure the people who sign documents are who they say they are. You'll also be responsible for checking that the signer isn't being forced or intimidated into signing a document — and that they understand what they are signing. You may need to put the person under oath before they sign the document and have them declare, under penalty of perjury, that the information they've given you is correct.
