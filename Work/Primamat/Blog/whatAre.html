Ground Protection Mats, Defined

Ground protection mats are exactly what they sound like: mats that prevent damage to the ground underneath. PrimaMat offers an outstanding lineup of ground protection mats made from recycled plastic.

How Ground Protection Mats Work

Ground protection mats work by spreading out the pressure of heavy machinery and foot traffic. This keeps equipment from being mired in the ground underneath and prevents damage to the machinery and the ground itself. Mats also provide traction and stability.

The Purpose of Ground Protection Mats

Since the purpose of ground protection mats is to prevent damage, they're appropriate for a variety of equipment. If a piece of machinery runs on the ground, it can probably benefit from a ground protection mat. Examples of equipment that utilize mats include:

  • Bucket trucks
  • Bulldozers
  • Cranes
  • Dump trucks
  • Excavators
  • Pole setters
  • Semi-tractors and trailers
  • Skid steers
  • Trenchers
  • Water trucks

How to Select Ground Protection Mats

Choosing the right ground protection mats can seem tricky, but breaking it down into simple steps can help. Here's what you need to consider:

  • Ground Type and Conditions. Different mats work better on different types of ground.
  • Load-Bearing Requirements. Mats have different load-bearing capacities, so make sure to pick ones that can handle the weight of whatever will be on top of them.
  • Size and Coverage Area. Mats come in various sizes, so measure the space and calculate how many mats you'll need.
  • Connection Mechanisms. The connection method can affect how stable the mats are and how easy they are to install and remove.
  • Durability and Lifespan. For long-term use, investing in high-quality, durable mats can save money in the long run.
  • Budget Considerations. Ground protection mats come in a range of prices.
