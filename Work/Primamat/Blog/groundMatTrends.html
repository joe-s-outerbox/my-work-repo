Frequently Asked Questions About Ground Protection Mats

As a trusted provider, PrimaMat fields many questions about ground protection mats. Here are some of the common questions we receive.

How do ground protection mats work?

The mats work by distributing the weight of heavy machinery, vehicles, or foot traffic over a larger surface area. This reduces the pressure on the ground, preventing it from getting damaged or turning into mud. The mats also provide a stable and non-slip surface, improving safety for workers and equipment.

Can ground protection mats be used in all weather conditions?

We design our ground protection mats for use in various weather conditions, including rain, snow, and extreme heat. They are made from materials that resist water, UV rays, and temperature fluctuations, ensuring they remain effective and durable regardless of the weather.

Are ground protection mats reusable?

Absolutely! One of the critical benefits of PrimaMat ground protection mats is their reusability. We build them to perform multiple times across different projects and events. With proper care and maintenance, these mats can provide long-term ground protection.
