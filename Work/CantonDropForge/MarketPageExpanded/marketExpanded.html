What Materials Are Used in Custom Forging?

We work with various materials, including carbon steel, alloy steel, stainless steel, titanium and other specialized alloys, to meet the diverse needs of our customers across different industries.

How Does Custom Forging Ensure Part Quality and Reliability?

Our custom forging process includes rigorous quality control measures, from material selection to final inspection, ensuring that each component meets the highest strength, durability and performance standards.

Can Canton Drop Forge Handle High-Volume Orders for Custom Forged Parts?

Yes, we are equipped to handle high-volume production runs and low-volume custom orders, with the flexibility to scale operations to meet customer demand while maintaining quality and delivery timelines.

What Differentiates Custom Forging from Other Metalworking Processes?

Custom forging is unique in its ability to produce components with unparalleled strength, structural integrity and durability due to deforming metal under high pressure, enhancing its mechanical properties and grain structure.

How Does the Custom Forging Process Work for New Projects?

The process begins with understanding the client's requirements, followed by material selection, design and engineering consultation. We then begin custom forging the part to shape, performing precision machining, testing and inspection to ensure the part meets the application's specific needs.
