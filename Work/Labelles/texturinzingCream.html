How Much Grooming Cream Should I Use for My Hair?

It depends on the look you're going for. If you want to rock that laid-back, just-rolled-out-of-bed look or a casual slick back, use a small amount, about the size of a dime. Add a little more grooming cream for a polished side part, comb it through and you've got a classic style that never fails to impress. Want a textured top? Work it in, spike it up or create waves, adding more if needed. It's all about finding the right balance for your hair type and style.

Can Short Hair Styling Products Be Used on Damp Hair?

Absolutely! Applying texturizing cream to slightly damp hair can help distribute the product evenly, giving you a more natural look.

How Long Does the Hold Last?

The hold is designed to last throughout the day, but it's easy to rework with a bit of water if you need to restyle. When you're ready to remove the grooming cream, a regular shampooing will do the trick. It washes out easily without leaving residue.
