Best Soap Dispenser FAQs

Below, we've answered a few common questions about the best soap dispensers at The Polished Jar. Please contact us if you'd like more information.

Can Any Liquid Soap Be Used in the Dispenser?

Most standard hand soaps, dishwashing liquids, and luxurious foaming soaps can be used in these dispensers. However, avoid using Castile soap and soaps with high amounts of lye, chlorine, or essential oils, as they can clog the pumps or deteriorate metal parts. Please note: If you're using foaming soap, you'll need to order the foaming pump to go with your dispenser.

What Customization Options Do You Offer?

We can customize your soap dispenser with printing that appears right on the bottle – or we can print and affix a waterproof label to your dispenser. You can add your name, a brief message or a special date to make your dispenser unique. If you order a white glass dispenser, you also have the option of engraving your text on the bottle.

How Should I Clean My Dispenser?

To maintain the beauty and longevity of your dispenser, gently wipe it with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners. A mild soap solution helps remove any soap residue or fingerprints.

Is It Easy to Refill the Soap Dispenser?

Yes, the dispensers are designed for easy and mess-free refilling. Use one of our funnels to make the process even smoother and ensure hassle-free refills.
