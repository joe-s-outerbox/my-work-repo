How Do I Ensure the Durability of My Solid Wood Desk?

ROMI Design's solid wood desks are built to last for generations. To ensure their longevity, it is essential to care for them properly. Regularly clean your desk with a soft, damp cloth, and avoid placing it in direct sunlight or areas with extreme humidity. Additionally, use coasters or pads to protect the surface from scratches or water damage.
