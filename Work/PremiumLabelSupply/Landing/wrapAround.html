How Do I Apply Wrap-Around Labels?

Make sure the surface you're applying the label to is clean and free of debris. Peel the label off the backing, align it properly (without touching the surface), and then place one end of the label on the product. Working toward the other end of the label, carefully secure it to the surface.

Why Should You Choose a Wrap-Around Label?

Wrap-around labels are more cost-effective and simpler than other labeling options. Unlike shrink wrap labels, which require expensive materials and precise heat, wrap-around labels adhere directly to your product, providing more durability.
