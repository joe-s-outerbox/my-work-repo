What’s the difference between men’s casual boots and dress boots?

Dress boots for men feature sleek silhouettes, polished leather, and refined details suited for formal or business settings. Men’s casual boots lean toward relaxed styling, often with softer leathers, suede, or rugged soles, making them ideal for everyday wear.
