How Do You Clean Tesla Floor Mats?

Cleaning Tesla floor mats is straightforward. First, remove the mats from your vehicle and shake off any loose debris. Use a mild detergent mixed with water and scrub the mats gently with a brush. After scrubbing, rinse them thoroughly to remove any soap residue and let them air dry completely before placing them back in your car. Regular cleaning helps maintain the mats' durability and appearance​​.
