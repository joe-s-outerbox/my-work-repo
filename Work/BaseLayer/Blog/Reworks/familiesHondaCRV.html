When it comes to the Midsize Crossover SUV, there are few that have the overall level of popularity that the Honda CR-V has. There are a bunch of these on the market, and if you google ‘Midsize Crossover SUV’ or ‘CUV’ you will see more than a dozen vehicles that bill themselves as such. But none have the staying power that the Honda CR-V has. Now the question is: why? Why does this SUV stand out from the crowd? Well that’s the question we are here to answer!

3 Reasons Why the Honda CR-V is Perfect for Families

Our team could spend hours talking about the performance of the CR-V but we don’t want to get too technical. We could discuss its spacious leg room and storage capacity, but those aren’t the main traits that make it a great vehicle (although they are nice to have). At the end of the day, there are 3 factors that truly make the CR-V a perfect fit for families:

Fuel Economy

We know we said that we wouldn’t be getting technical, but with gas prices increasing on an every other day basis, this is an important part. Gas is a major expense for families, especially ones that have kids in sports (which is a CR-V specialty), and the CR-V does a great job of conserving gas without sacrificing performance.

Aggressive Styling

While we are sitting back and worrying about gas mileage, we still want to look cool, and that is another thing that the CR-V does well. With aggressive styling that counters its competitors, the Honda CR-V is where you go when you want to see what rugged looks like when matched up with luxury. It is a showstopper to be sure.

Reliability Is King

They say it all the time in sports: “the greatest ability is availability.” The Honda CR-V certainly embodies this ideal. Throughout its run, the CR-V has had an absurdly low amount of complaints lodged against it. Since the redesign of the CR-V in 2017, there has not been a crossover SUV with less Registered Complaints.
